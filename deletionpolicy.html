Second Shelf Data Deletion Policy

This policy outlines the process for users to request the deletion of their account and associated data within the Second Shelf app. We understand the importance of user privacy and are committed to providing you with control over your data.

Information We Collect

Second Shelf collects the following user data:

  • Email Address (used for user authentication)
  • Phone Number (optional, if provided by the user)
  • City (optional, if provided by the user)

Data Deletion Process

If you wish to delete your Second Shelf account and associated data, you can follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us:** Send an email to kunalkhairnar2005@gmail.com with the subject line "Request Account Deletion."
  2. Verification:** In the email, include your registered email address associated with your Second Shelf account. This helps us verify your identity and ensure the correct account is deleted.

Deletion Timeframe

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 business days of receiving your email. Once your request is processed, your account and associated data (email address, phone number, and city) will be deleted from our systems.

Data Retention (Optional)

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy or the data deletion process, please feel free to contact us at kunalkhairnar2005@gmail.com.

Updates

We may update this Data Deletion Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy within the app or on our website.
